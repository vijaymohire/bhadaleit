### 5. [`program_management_office/`](program_management_office/)
> **Objective:** Define and manage the **strategic alignment and oversight** of all QAI programs.

Focus:
- Multi-project program governance and KPI tracking

- Risk management and executive reporting

### 6. [`project_delivery_readiness/`](project_delivery_readiness/)
> **Objective:** Validate and certify project readiness before deployment.

Includes:

- Collaboration tools and delivery analytics
